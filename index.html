Building Design Process

The building design process is a complex and multifaceted endeavor that involves several key stages. It typically begins with the initial concept and feasibility study, where architects and engineers assess the project's viability. This is followed by schematic design, where preliminary plans are created, and design development, where details are refined. The construction documents phase involves creating detailed drawings and specifications for contractors. Finally, the project moves into the construction phase, where the design is brought to life on-site.
